About Our Archive

VHS Mania started as a collection of unusual and unexplained recordings discovered in abandoned storage units, estate sales, and mysterious packages. Our archive preserves these artifacts of the analog era that might otherwise be lost to time.

From strange broadcasts to inexplicable home videos, we maintain these recordings for researchers and the curious to examine. Many of our tapes contain footage that defies conventional explanation.
